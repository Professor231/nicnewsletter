A STORY OF POSSIBILITY

Imagine Kebbeh, a small cocoa farm owner producing chocolate products. Despite the rich taste of her chocolates, her sales were very low. She tried printing flyers and relying on word of mouth, but the world was moving faster. Customers were chatting on WhatsApp, browsing Instagram, googling businesses, and asking for online catalogs. Kebbeh realized her competition wasn’t just the store across the street but all the brands online.

She thought: “Maybe the digital game is only for big players like Lonestar MTN or Orange that have money for advertising.” But in truth, today’s digital world is built for every business willing to step in. Even $5 can make a difference.

WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOUR BUSINESS?

1.84 million people in Liberia are now online — that’s 32.4% of the population. At the same time, there are 5.11 million mobile connections, covering about 90% of the population, with most devices supporting 3G, 4G, or even 5G. The stage is set: the infrastructure is here, devices are in people’s hands, and every year more customers are shifting to digital platforms.

For your business, this is a major opportunity. You can launch a website that works like a 24/7 storefront, stay visible on social media, and use content management to share updates consistently. You can also run lead generation campaigns and invest in quality visuals that instantly help your brand stand out.

Digital isn’t optional anymore — it’s the new marketplace.
